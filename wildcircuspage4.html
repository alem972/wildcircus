Trapeze artists


Charlene, Anna and Samantha will interpret aerobatics for you.
This act wich will surprise you. Each one of their acts is unique.

Simply hooked to their trapezes and ribbons by their legs and feet, they will perform stunts in the air with only security experience.

Suspended 10 meters from the ground, they will maintain the suspense until the highlight.

These three friends for 12 years, have developed a visible complicity in life and on the circus ring.
