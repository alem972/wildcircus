Artists


The Wild Circus was created in 1970 by Arnold Green when he was 50 years with his wife and his 2 children: Jean and Paul.

In 2003, following the death of Arnold, his 25 children took over the circus and have integrated new acts more sensational.

Now, the Wild Circus family has widened:


Jean married and he had a son, Carl.
Jean and Carl are 2 of our 4 clowns.


Paul married too and he had a daughter:
Anna, a trapeze artist.


Carl, a clown,

and Camille,our tamer, got married and they have a son and a daughter:
John a clown

and Charlene a trapeze artist.


David, the knives thrower,

and Marie, his assistant and wife, integrated the Wild Circus in 2004.


They have a daughter, Samantha the trapeze artist.
