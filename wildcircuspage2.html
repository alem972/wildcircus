The knifes throwing and his assistant

Let yourself by one of the most dangerous act ofour show: The knife thrower!
David, our knifes thrower, will impress you with his knives and axes.
David doesn't throw it all on a simple target, but on his wonderful assistant Marie.

Their act consist of 2 parts:

During the first part, Marie will be attached to a board,David will throw knives and axes on the board without touching Marie.


For the second part, Marie held various objects in her hands and on her head, David will target with his sharp knives.


Marie and David will brave all dangers under your applause.
